Filipino Recipe Corner - Site Plan

Site Name

Filipino Recipe Corner – A site that showcases top Filipino recipes.

Site Purpose

Provide information about the top 3 Filipino recipes, including images, descriptions, ingredients, and preparation steps. The site aims to introduce Filipino cuisine, making it easy for people to learn and cook from home.

Scenarios

  • What are some popular Filipino dishes I can cook for my family?
  • How do I make chicken adobo or other traditional Filipino meals?
  • What ingredients do I need to cook the top Filipino dishes?
  • Are these recipes beginner-friendly?

Color Schema

  • Rust Red (#9E2A2B): Headings, site name, links
  • Light Beige (#F9F3E3): Background areas and text sections
  • Forest Green (#315B3C): Buttons, links, and accents

Typography

  • Headings: Lora, serif
  • Body Text: Roboto, sans-serif
